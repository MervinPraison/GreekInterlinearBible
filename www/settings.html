  • English Transliteration

English Transliteration takes the selected Greek text and converts the Greek characters into characters English speakers are more familiar with. For example, the Greek "Alpha" would become the English "a". There are multiple methods to do this, some more readable than others. This program uses one which hopefully makes the text reasonably readable.
